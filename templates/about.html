ABOUT US

Welcome to Hot Or Not, the ultimate destination for those who want to have their looks judged by millions of people around the world. Our website is dedicated to helping people find out just how attractive they really are by submitting their photos to our community for voting. At Hot Or Not, we believe that everyone is beautiful in their own way, and we're here to celebrate that beauty. Whether you're a supermodel or just an average person, we want to give you a chance to shine and receive recognition for your looks. Our voting system is simple and straightforward. Users submit their photos, and the community votes on their attractiveness. You can also browse through other people's photos and vote on them as well. We believe in fairness and objectivity, so our voting system is completely anonymous, and we do not tolerate any form of bullying or harassment. Hot Or Not has been around for over a decade, and we're proud to say that we've helped countless people boost their self-esteem and feel more confident about their looks. We've even had some of our users go on to become successful models, actors, and influencers. So whether you're looking for validation, a confidence boost, or just some fun, come join our community at Hot Or Not. Who knows, you might just be the hottest person on the website!
